About the Artist

I’m Élise Maron, a ceramicist drawing inspiration from forest walks, mountain textures, and seaside driftwood. Each piece I shape is an homage to the earth — organic, imperfect, yet deeply harmonious.

Using natural clays and glazes crafted from local minerals, my studio practice honors the slow rhythm of handmade creation. You’re invited to bring a piece of nature into your home.
